z doesn't do anything on its own. But it pairs with a number of other things. It is used for redrawing the screen, saving and quitting, as well as manipulating folds.

Here we show z+ and how it redraws the first line out of sight at the bottom of the window to the top of the window. This is a full page down movement.

z- will redraw the cursor line to the bottom of the window.
zt will redraw the cursor line to the top of the window zz and z. will redraw the cursor line to the middle of the window zb will redraw the cursor line to the bottom of the window
