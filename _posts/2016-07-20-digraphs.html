Digraphs allow you to insert characters not found on a standard keyboard. The full list of available digraphs can be found by typing :dig
To use a digraph, type ctrl-k while in insert mode and then enter the two characters. At the very least, it can save you from googling a unicode character so you can cut and paste it.
